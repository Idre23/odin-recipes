Description

This classic lasagna is perfect comfort food. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted chesse, it´s a hearty and satisfying dish great for family dinners or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 800g canned tomatos
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  • Grated mozzarella or parmesan

Steps

  1. Chop onion and garlic, sauté in oil.
  2. Add beef, cook until browned
  3. Stir in tomato paste and canned tomatos, simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Make béchamel
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and béchamel
  6. Top with cheese and bake at 180°C for 40 mins.
  7. Let rest for 10 mins before serving
