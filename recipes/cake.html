Description

This rich chocolate cake is soft, moist, and packes with deep cocoa flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 75 cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp backing powder
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 240ml milk
  • 120ml oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 240ml boiling water
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix dry Ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Carefully stir in boiling water.
  5. Pour into greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Let coolcompletly before serving.
